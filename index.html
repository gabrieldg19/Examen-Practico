FAQ

How does the 30-day free trial work?

You can use Pulse completely free for 30 days. After this period, the credit card you used to sign up will be charged. If you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be charged anything.

If I upgrade an existing account, do I get another free trial?

Only new accounts are eligible for the 30-day free trial. If you upgrade an existing account, the new amount will be charged on your next billing cycle date.

What is a "Financial Account"?

Financial Accounts are groupings of income and expenses that can be used for tracking or reporting. They are flexible and can be used to differentiate between bank accounts, businesses, individuals, or between personal and business records.

Do I have to commit to a long-term contract?

No. There is no mandatory contract when using Pulse, you only need to agree to the Terms of Service.

Can I cancel my account if I don't use Pulse?

Yes, you can cancel your account at any time. To do so, go to the billing page and click "Cancel My Account" at the bottom. Only the account owner can perform the cancellation.

What payment types do you accept?

We accept online payments with Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express.
